Insurance & Medicare Coverage

Most wheelchair repairs are covered by insurance when medically necessary

Medicare Part B

Covers 80% of approved wheelchair repairs after deductible. Repairs covered up to replacement cost.

Medicaid

Illinois Medicaid covers wheelchair repairs with prior authorization for repairs over $500.

Private Insurance

Most private plans cover medically necessary repairs. Check with your provider for details.

Veterans Affairs

VA covers repairs for equipment issued through VA medical centers and approved vendors.
